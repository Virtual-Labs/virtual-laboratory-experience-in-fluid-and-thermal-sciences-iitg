Description of the Apparatus:-

The apparatus is shown diagrammatically in fig.1 , consists of two separate hydraulic circuits, one painted dark blue , one painted light blue , each containing a number of pipe system components . Both circuits are supplied with water from the same hydraulic bench. The components in each of the circuits are as follows:

Dark Blue Circuit: -

1. Gate Valve

2. Standard Elbow

3. 90° Mitre Bend

4. Straight Pipe

Light Blue Circuit:

5. Glove Valve

6. Sudden Expansion

7. Sudden Contraction

8. 150 mm 90° Radius Bend

9. 100 mm 90° Radius Bend

10. 50 mm 90° Radius Bend


























Fig 1 Diagrammatic Arrangement of the apparatus


Key to Apparatus Arrangement 

A- Straight pipe 13.7 mm Bore

B -90o Sharp Bend (Mitre)

C-Proprietary 90o Elbow

D-Gate Valve

E -Sudden Enlargement – 13.7 mm / 26.4 mm

F -Sudden Contraction – 26.4 mm / 13.7 mm

G -Smooth 90o Bend 50 mm Radius

H -Smooth 90o Bend 100 mm Radius

I -Smooth 90o Bend 150 mm Radius

J -Globe Valve 

K -Straight Pipe 26.4 mm Bore 

In all cases (except the gate and globe valves) the pressure change across each of the components is measured by a pair of pressurized piezometer tubes. In the case of the valves pressure measurement is made by U-tubes containing mercury
